## Between Glastonbury and the Jurassic Coast

Location matters. When you're liberating the elements within yourself, the land you're on becomes part of the work.

### North End Farm

A thatched farmhouse dating to before 1600, with parts rebuilt after a fire in 1640. Lovingly restored and maintained - charm and character combined with modern amenities. Stone walls, Tudor fireplace, old bread oven.

Set in secluded Dorset countryside with beautiful outdoor space for walking and sitting. [Link to farmhouse details]

### The Landscape

You're in ancient sacred territory. Within an hour: Stanton Drew Stone Circles (3000-2000 BCE), third largest in England. Priddy Circles on the Mendips, four Bronze Age henges at the highest point of the plateau. Burial mounds, standing stones, hillforts throughout. This land has been recognized as sacred for five thousand years.

Two miles away, the Jurassic Coast - England's only natural UNESCO World Heritage Site. 185 million years of Earth's history visible in the cliffs. Walk here and you're literally walking through deep time. Red sandstone, chalk downs, clay beds full of fossils. You can watch water and wind actively reshaping ancient rock.

Forty-five minutes: Glastonbury. Earth's heart chakra. The Tor rising from flat Somerset Levels. Chalice Well with its red spring. The Michael and Mary ley lines intersecting. Abbey ruins where tradition says the first Christian church in Europe was built. People have been drawn here for millennia because something about this landscape opens the heart.

### Elements Alive in the Land

Insights come. From sitting by a stone circle at dusk. Walking the coastal path in mist. Standing in a field, allowing yourself to simply be present.

We'll take time for this. Space for solo walking, sitting outside, learning how to be with the land in your own way to receive what is needed for you in that moment. Group practices outdoors when weather permits. The landscape becomes part of our circle, part of what teaches and holds us.

When you're working to discover how the elements move uniquely through you, being in a place where their power has been honored for millennia matters. The land holds what you're ready to find.

## Who This Training Is For

This training is designed for practitioners who sense they have gifts to discover and offer. When you serve your own consciousness, you serve the whole. This is about finding deep satisfaction in the work - not through searching for the next thing, but through stepping into what's already yours.

You'll benefit most if you:

**Have an established practice.** You've been teaching, healing, or facilitating long enough to know what you don't know. You've encountered the limits of what technique alone can offer. You're ready to develop the inner capacity that makes good work great work.

**Are ready to deepen your relationship with practice.** You're learning to show up from self-appreciation rather than discipline, from genuine care for yourself rather than forcing yourself through the motions.

**Understand that depth takes time.** You're not looking for quick fixes or three-easy-step formulas. You're willing to stay with a practice, with a question, with discomfort long enough for real transformation to occur.

**Want to serve from authenticity rather than performance.** You're ready to discover the gifts you actually hold and claim them fully. Real authenticity means manifesting those gifts at their highest potential - serving from what's uniquely yours, not from borrowed ideas of what you should be.

**Recognize that embodiment is the path.** You understand that intellectual knowledge, while valuable, isn't the same as lived wisdom. You're willing to make the practices your own rather than just repeating what you've been taught.

**Feel called to serve.** This work matters to you. You facilitate or teach because something in you is called to offer your gifts for the evolution of the collective. You're looking for ways to deepen that offering.

**Come from any tradition or background.** While this training draws from yogic traditions (mainly Kundalini Yoga) and energy healing work, you don't need to be a yoga teacher to benefit. The elemental framework and practices we'll explore are universal. Whether you work as a therapist, coach, healer, bodyworker, meditation teacher, or any other form of transformational facilitator, this training will deepen your capacity to serve.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to be a yoga teacher to apply?**

No. While this training draws from yogic traditions (particularly Kundalini Yoga) and energy healing work, it's designed for anyone who holds space for transformation - teachers, healers, coaches, therapists, bodyworkers, facilitators of any kind. The elemental framework and practices are universal and can be integrated into whatever work you do.

**What if I'm not familiar with Kundalini Yoga?**

That's perfectly fine. While we'll use some Kundalini Yoga practices and concepts, you don't need prior experience with this tradition. The focus is on working with universal principles (the elements, energy, embodiment) that exist across all spiritual and healing traditions. If you have a committed practice of some kind, you'll be able to engage with this work.

**What if I can't attend all five modules?**

The training is designed as a complete path, with each module building on previous work. However, Module One can be taken as a standalone intensive. If you're uncertain about the full commitment, you can begin with the first module and decide about continuing afterward.

**What's the daily schedule like?**

Days begin with morning practice around 6:00 AM, followed by breakfast and teaching sessions throughout the day. Afternoons typically include practice, study, and time for integration. Evenings offer group discussions and closing practices. The schedule balances structure with flexibility, intensity with rest.

**What should I bring?**

A yoga mat, meditation cushion or bench, comfortable clothing for practice, layers for varying temperatures, journal and pens, any props you prefer for your practice, and an open heart. Detailed packing information will be sent upon registration.

**How does this differ from other advanced trainings?**

This isn't a certification program in the traditional sense. It's an immersion training focused on deepening your own practice and your capacity to serve from that depth. While it will enhance your facilitation skills, the primary focus is inner work and embodiment rather than curriculum and technique.

**Will I receive a certificate?**

Upon completion of all five modules, you'll receive a certificate of completion from this training program. This represents your commitment to advanced study and depth work in spiritual practice and transformational facilitation.

**What if I have physical limitations or health concerns?**

Please contact me directly to discuss. The practices can often be modified to work with different body needs, but it's important we talk about your specific situation before you register.

**Can I arrive early or stay late?**

The farmhouse may be available for an extra night before or after the training week. Please inquire about availability when registering.
